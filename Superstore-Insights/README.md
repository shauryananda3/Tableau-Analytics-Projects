# 🛒 Superstore Insights Analytics

A professional Tableau analytics project designed to evaluate retail business performance across sales, profit, quantity sold, customer segments, product categories, shipping modes, and state-wise operations.

This dashboard helps retail leaders, category managers, and analysts understand profitability trends, optimize product mix, improve logistics efficiency, and support data-driven growth decisions.

---

# 📌 Business Objective

Retail organizations need clear visibility into revenue performance, category profitability, customer segments, and operational efficiency to improve margins and scale growth.

This dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- Analyze sales, profit, and quantity trends  
- Compare state-wise business performance  
- Monitor category and sub-category profitability  
- Evaluate customer segment contribution  
- Assess shipping mode efficiency  
- Support strategic planning using analytics

# 🔍 Key Insights (Based on Dashboard)

## 💰 Revenue & Profit Insights

- Total **Sales reached $2.3M** with **Profit of $286.4K**, indicating healthy overall business performance.  
- Profit trends showed strong upward momentum toward later periods.  
- Quantity sold reached **37,873 units**, reflecting strong order volume.  
- Revenue growth outpaced some profit periods, indicating margin fluctuation.

## 🌍 State Performance Insights

- **California** ranked as the top-performing state by profit.  
- **New York** and **Washington** followed closely as high-value markets.  
- Several lower-tier states delivered minimal contribution, suggesting optimization potential.  
- State concentration indicates dependence on a few major markets.

## 🛍 Category Insights

- **Office Supplies** generated the highest overall profit.  
- **Technology** was the second strongest contributor with strong margin potential.  
- **Furniture** underperformed significantly compared to other categories.

## 📦 Sub-Category Insights

- **Accessories** ranked highest in profitability.  
- **Binders, Paper, Copiers, and Storage** delivered consistent returns.  
- **Tables** ranked lowest, indicating margin pressure or discounting issues.  
- **Bookcases and Supplies** also showed weaker profitability.

## 👥 Customer Segment Insights

- **Consumer segment** generated the largest profit share.  
- **Corporate segment** was the second largest contributor.  
- **Home Office** remained smaller but stable.

## 🚚 Shipping Insights

- **Standard Class** was the most profitable shipping mode by a wide margin.  
- **Second Class** and **First Class** performed moderately.  
- **Same Day** shipping delivered the lowest profitability.

# 💡 Specific Recommendations (Dashboard Driven)

## Revenue Growth

- Increase investment in top-performing states like California, New York, and Washington.  
- Expand profitable Office Supplies and Technology promotions.  
- Create targeted campaigns for Consumer segment customers.

## Margin Improvement

- Reassess pricing strategy for **Furniture**, especially Tables.  
- Reduce excessive discounting on weak sub-categories.  
- Improve contribution from lower-performing states through localized pricing.

## Logistics Optimization

- Promote **Standard Class** shipping where feasible to protect margins.  
- Use premium shipping only for high-value orders.  
- Review cost structure of Same Day shipping.

## Product Portfolio Strategy

- Increase inventory for Accessories, Binders, Copiers, and Paper.  
- Rationalize low-performing SKUs in Tables and Bookcases.  
- Bundle Technology accessories with core products for upsell.

## Customer Strategy

- Launch loyalty campaigns for Consumer segment.  
- Build B2B pricing plans for Corporate customers.  
- Introduce premium workspace solutions for Home Office users.
